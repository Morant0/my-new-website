Summary Liangda Fang received his Ph.D. from Sun Yat-sen University in 2015. After graduation, he joined Jinan University as a lecturer and was promoted to associate professor in 2018. His main research interests include artificial intelligence, knowledge representation and reasoning, machine learning, planning, multi-agent systems, and educational artificial intelligence. He has published 35 papers in CCF recommended A/B class conferences and journals.
